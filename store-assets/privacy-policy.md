# Lumina Privacy Policy

*Last updated: [DATE]*

## Overview

Lumina ("we", "our", or "the extension") is an educational browser extension that helps students learn through guided questions. This policy explains how we handle your information.

## Information We Collect

### Conversation Content
- Messages you send to Lumina
- Text you highlight on web pages when asking for help

### Technical Data
- Access code (to verify you have permission to use Lumina)
- Browser session identifier (temporary, for conversation continuity)

## How We Use Your Information

Your conversation content is:
1. Sent to our secure server
2. Processed by Claude AI to generate helpful responses
3. Temporarily stored for conversation continuity (max 1 hour)
4. Then permanently deleted

We use this information ONLY to:
- Provide tutoring assistance
- Maintain conversation context within a session

## What We DON'T Do

- ❌ Sell your data
- ❌ Share your data with third parties (except Claude AI for processing)
- ❌ Store conversations permanently
- ❌ Track your browsing history
- ❌ Use your data for advertising
- ❌ Collect personal information (name, email, etc.)

## Data Storage

- **Access codes**: Stored locally in your browser
- **Conversations**: Temporarily stored on our server, deleted after 1 hour of inactivity
- **No permanent storage**: We don't maintain user accounts or long-term data

## Third-Party Services

We use Anthropic's Claude AI to process your messages. Their privacy policy: https://www.anthropic.com/privacy

## Children's Privacy

Lumina is designed for students ages 13-16. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent. Access codes should be distributed by parents or educators.

## Security

- All data transmitted over HTTPS
- API keys stored securely on our server
- No sensitive data stored in the browser extension

## Your Rights

You can:
- Clear your local data by removing the extension
- Request deletion of any server-side data by contacting us
- Stop using Lumina at any time

We may update this policy occasionally. Changes will be posted here with a new "last updated" date.
